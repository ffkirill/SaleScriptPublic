What is conversations (sales) scripts

Scripts of conversations, often called sales scripts - a detailed scenario of conversation on the phone. It significantly reduces the risk of losing a client, both potential and existing related management incompetence, stress factors, and helps to learn to a high standard talking on the phone a new member of your team, reducing the training period. By implementing a scripts of calls to your business, you can after a short period of internship (1-2 weeks) to set the salaries of employees, according to its real value for the company and it certainly will not be offended, because there will be quite confident. Depending on the purpose for which designed call script, they can be used for:

  • Cold calls to organizations and to individuals
  • Handling incoming calls
  • Customer Service
  • Gathering information about competitors
  • Re-sale calls
  • Poll of satisfaction on the cooperation

And not limited to these. After all, you can virtually eliminate the factor of chance and incompetence of the employees in any communication with the client. Employees on the phone is the face of your company, in fact, they often give the impression of it

What sales script should include

Depending on the purpose, the script composition may be different. The main thing that you need to achieve - is the goal of a particular scenario. In one case, it may be a sale, in another - the removal of emotional intensity (support staff). You have to determine for themselves the link from which you will get the maximum conversion to sale, the solution customer problems and so on.

The main sections of high-quality sales script may include:

  • The conversation with the secretary, identify the person making the decision (DM) and getting in touch with him
  • Detection of the needs of decision-makers in your product/service
  • Product Presentation
  • Work with objections
  • Closing oo the target of the script
  • Additional sale

You should monitor your managers, listen to the recording of calls, create checklists on the main points for the best results. By implementing sales scripts you can dramatically improve your sales. Who wants to get rich?
